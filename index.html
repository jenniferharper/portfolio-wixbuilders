In 2019 my logo design and branding concept was chosen to represent a new digital product incentive.

Providing struggling Wix platform users with the opportunity to hire a professional to build their website, this product required a friendly, approachable, professional brand identity.

This page showcases the brand I developed for the product. Fun UI elements, soft curves, bright colors, and happy customers give a sense of a fun, modern, creative company, easing the customer into trusting the brand, and giving credibility within the current available market.
